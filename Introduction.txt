Hello everyone!

The creation of the 【Chinese Revised Common Lectionary (RCL) for Google Calendar】 was born out of the intersection of my three very different backgrounds:

📖 I graduated from China Evangelical Seminary (CES) and am currently continuing my studies at Chinese Lutheran Seminary, deeply rooted in the Lutheran faith tradition. This has given me a fundamental understanding of and need for solid biblical study and the rhythm of the Revised Common Lectionary (RCL). I truly know the beauty of anchoring daily devotions in the church calendar.

💻 As a programmer, I noticed a massive gap in the Chinese-speaking world: there wasn't an automated, accurate, and seamlessly integrated "Chinese RCL for Google Calendar" for modern Christians. Since I couldn't find one, I decided to code it into existence myself! This system precisely calculates and generates a Chinese RCL calendar that perfectly aligns with universal church liturgical standards.

🎸 At the same time, serving as a worship leader for many years, I know firsthand the spiritual depth that comes from pairing daily devotions and Sunday service prep with music that echoes the current liturgical season and daily scriptures.

Because I happen to have this unique blend of backgrounds, I am incredibly excited to share this carefully crafted 【Chinese RCL for Google Calendar】 with you all. Whether you are in the US, Asia, or anywhere else, please feel free to share this with your Chinese-speaking Christian friends, pastors, and church networks worldwide! I hope it becomes a wonderful blessing to global churches, preachers, worship teams, and brothers and sisters everywhere.
